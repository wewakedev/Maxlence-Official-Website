Our mission was to redesign a website that reflects all the services that TPR offers. TPR maily focuses on luxrury services. With a lof of competition in the market, standing out with luxury brand and most imprtantly creating brand awareness was one of the top challenges for TPR. Establishing & maintaing trust with customers is always a critical factor for long term success. As a result, the Maxlence team had to come up with minimal yet aesthetically luxurious website that conveyed people the brand value and identity, the types of service TPR provides and area they serve in. Based on this information & user research, we were able to resolve this challenge in the new website design.

Discover

The stakeholders were involved in every ideation & brainstorming workshop. This ensured that the users’ needs were taken care of without a disconnect. Maxlence team believed that identifying target audience was a powerful technique to get insights of what they want. We analysed competition by conducting a research & gained knowledge regarding what best suits our project. This helped us build a website that gained a visibility among users.

Design

Based on the research & our understanding of TPR, the maxlence team followed a comprehensive design process where :

  • We started with journey maps which is a detailed visualisation that shows how a user-based persona is feeling through out the process of selecting and registering for the service.
  • We then created task flows to imply visualise the motivations.
  • We defined, understood & built a simple & adoptable information architecture.

Wireframes

Initially we had a concept of bold design which client had rejected as TPR wanted to focus on luxury services and so wanted a website which has elegant and luxurious aesthetics. Then we focused on simpler and scalable wireframes to understand the benefits of the services offered. This would help users to differentiate and book required services effortlessly.

Design

The designs imply the intrinsic values of TPR – dynamic and progressive. Overall, the visual design is created from a perspective to build trust among customers, reduce drop-off rate and increase revenue. The website is very simple, elegant which conveys the value proposition offered and helps customers with easy and convenient navigation. We carefully crafted content for each page to build trust and credibility and convince new users to book services available. The dynamic yet flexible screens are designed to convey the benefits and allow the platform to be scalable and simplified to improve the experience of the users.
